Know More!

In a bold move to understand the remote workforce dynamics and to reverse talent migration to major cities, Mahendra NextWealth is launching a comprehensive survey targeting workforce that is either working from work-from-home or hybrid model. This initiative aims to shed light on the key demographics and domain expertise within the remote talent pool, while also solidifying its commitment to decentralizing opportunities and empowering individuals to work from their native towns and villages.

However, the initiative goes beyond mere data collection. Mahendra NextWealth is motivated by a larger vision: countering the migration trend towards major cities. By understanding the demographics and expertise of the remote employees, the company aims to identify training programs and communication strategies to better support them.
